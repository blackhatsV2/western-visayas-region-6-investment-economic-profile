# Project Overview: Western Visayas Region 6 Investment Economic Profile

## 1. Introduction
The **Western Visayas Region 6 Investment Economic Profile** is a web-based digital platform designed to showcase the economic landscape, investment opportunities, and vital statistics of Region 6 in the Philippines.

## 2. Objective
The primary goal of this system is to attract domestic and foreign investors by providing them with easily accessible, up-to-date, and visually appealing economic data. It digitizes the traditional economic profile book into an interactive web experience and a downloadable PDF format.

## 3. Scope
The project covers:
- **Dynamic Content Management:** Allowing administrators to easily update texts, charts, statistical grids, and tables through a structured backend.
- **Interactive Data Presentation:** Frontend views displaying various economic indicators using different layout types (hero, list, chart, stats_grid, table).
- **PDF Generation:** Automated conversion of the online economic profile into a professionally formatted PDF document for offline reading.
- **Inquiry Management:** A communication channel for prospective investors to send inquiries directly to the region's economic development administrators.

## 4. Target Audience
- Foreign and Local Investors
- Business Development Agencies
- Government Officials & Policy Makers
- Researchers and Academics
- General Public interested in the regional economy

## 5. Key Technologies
* **Framework:** Laravel (PHP)
* **Database:** MySQL / SQLite
* **Frontend:** Blade Templating, HTML/CSS/JS
* **Export:** PDF Generation libraries
